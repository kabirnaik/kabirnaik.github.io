  • Quadcopter(Carbon Fibre Build)

My first project in aerial robotics. I am very exhilarated to apportion it with you guys. I made this project in the Winter of 2018, I was 15 years old and was more zealous about robotics than ever. If any of you seek any details other than the ones i've mentioned below, feel free to contact me via email. Danke.

Here are some links to the components I used, the pin diagram and connections are quite basic and there are many resources available online, so I won't mention them here :

1. Brushless Motors 2. Transmitter and Receiver 3. Carbon Fibre Frame 4. Power Distribution Board 5. F3 Flight Controller 6. 4*20A Optical ESCs 7. 14.8V Li-Po 1500mAh

NOW LETS COME TO CONFIGURING YOUR DRONE!!!!THE MOST CRUCIAL PART!!!

Caution before you configure you're drone:Always disconnect the battery while configuring you're drone

Now to configure there are many softwares, but i would recommend the BetaFlight-Configurator, as it's simple and easy to use. There are many tutorial on Youtube on the configuration so you can check them out too.

A Guide on How to Configure You're Drone.

Thanks For Reaching here, and all the best, if you wish to experiment with this project!!!!!

  • Electric Pennyboard

This was my first time making an electric skateboard and also one of my largest investments in terms of effort, time and money. This project was started in the summer of 2018(15 years old). I was lucky to have had certain services like welding available. So let's get into the details right away!!!

Here are the links to all the components I had used :

1. PU wheels(85A hardness) 2. 2 * 11.1V 5200mAh Li-Po Battery 3. Electric Skateboard Trucks 4. Voltage Tester (buzzer) 5. Brushless Motor 270KV 6. 180A Car ESC 7. Banana Connectors(If Required) 8. Penny Board

9. A Case to store the Circuit(I used a plastic tiffin)
